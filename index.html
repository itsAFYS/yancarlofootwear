ABOUT US

Learn more about our company

History

In 2013, Carlos Anturi (owner and founder of the company) related that the origin of the company dates back to a request from a client for the production of supplies. He discovered the passion he felt for the work and dedicated himself completely to that work

Age

The company has been working in the sector for 10 years, offering high-quality shoes and a variety of styles

Sector

The company works in the manufacturing sector, specifically manufacturing leather shoes

Products

Calzado Yancarlo has various styles, colors and designs in its range. However, it always offers the option of customizing footwear depending on the client's needs.

Mision

“We are a company focused on the manufacture of men's formal shoes with excellent quality that always seeks innovation in the market, the satisfaction of our customers and the production of our products in a sustainable way for the environment. With the best trained employees of the industry"

Vision

“By 2030, our men's formal footwear company will seek to continue innovating in the market, will be a leading brand in Bogotá and will carry out new designs based on the needs of our customers. The products will be designed with the best technologies in the range, which will allow an advantage in the level of competition with others in the sector.”
